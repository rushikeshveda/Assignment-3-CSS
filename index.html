Discover the Magic of Ferrero Rocher

Ferrero Rocher is a chocolate and hazelnut confectionery produced by the Italian chocolatier Ferrero. It consists of a whole roasted hazelnut encased in a thin wafer shell filled with hazelnut cream and covered in milk chocolate and chopped hazelnuts.
